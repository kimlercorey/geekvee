How America Gives (2012)

How America Gives is based on exact dollar amounts released by the Internal Revenue Service showing the value of charitable deductions claimed by American taxpayers. To provide a fair analysis, The Chronicle's rankings show the percentage of their income that households donated from the money they had left after paying their taxes and covering housing, food and other essential expenses.
